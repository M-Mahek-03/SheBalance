## 🎯 Presentation Tips

### Delivery
1. **Speak clearly** and maintain eye contact
2. **Show enthusiasm** - you believe in this!
3. **Use storytelling** - share artisan stories
4. **Demo smoothly** - practice multiple times
5. **Time management** - stick to 10 minutes

### Technical Demo
1. **Have backup** - video if live demo fails
2. **Test beforehand** - ensure everything works
3. **Show real data** - not lorem ipsum
4. **Highlight AI** - emphasize AWS services
5. **Show impact** - real metrics

### Q&A Preparation
**Expected Questions**:
1. How do you handle data privacy?
2. What's your go-to-market strategy?
3. How do you verify artisan skills?
4. What's your revenue model?
5. How do you scale to 10M users?
6. Why AWS over other cloud providers?
7. How do you handle multiple languages?
8. What's your customer acquisition cost?

**Prepare Answers** for each!
